Dean Student Welfare



The office of Dean Student Welfare ensures the overall growth & progression of the students in a conducive and constructive scholastic environment. It organizes various curricular activities to make the campus life vibrant and lively. At Glocal University we believe that extra-curricular activities play a vital role in the holistic development of students. Hence we are committed to encourage all such activities. The office of Dean Student Welfare functions as a hub of various activities that promotes fraternity, corporation, assimilation, accommodation and sense of brotherhood among students. The team of Students Welfare of theUniversity plays a key role to encourage the talent of students and has full-fledged inter-mediation between students and administration of the University.Our motto is to help & motivate our students and take care of them on University premises.DSW is one window that is open for all students to make their constructive journey, stimulate young minds, and bring outinquisitiveness, passion, and the thirst for acquiring versatility in the various frontiers of students life for the overall development of their personality as a conscientious citizen of our society. It is the constant efforts and contributions of students which play an important role in apprising students, originality, and unlocking new opportunities and creativity.

Chief Proctor



“There is no magic wand that can resolve our problems. The solution rests with our work and discipline.” – Jose Eduardo dos Santos Welcome to all students,I wish heartiest congratulations to you for joining Glocal University.As you are a member of this prestigious University, you are expected to be a sensible and disciplined student. “Discipline is the bridge between goals and accomplishment.” -Jim Rohn As the Chief Proctor of The Glocal University, it is my duty and the duty of all members of Proctorial Board to maintain discipline and decorum in the University Campus, but it cannot be achieved without your cooperation and coordination. Discipline plays an important role in the life of an individual person and group of persons. It is the path to a successful life and key to freedom from rigidity of thought and bad perspective of life. “For a man to conquer himself is the first and noblest of all victories.” – Plato We practice discipline in all the domains and expect the same from the students.The Glocal University encourages all the students to take part in the academics as well as extracurricular activities of the University. You are expected to spend quality time with your friends, interact with teachers, go to library and equip yourself with knowledge, maintaining the decorum and discipline within university campus.You may find some kind of restrictions in University Campus. Those restrictions are not shackles in the legs of the students rather they teach them how to be in limits, what to do and what not to do, and how to do anything. And that is discipline. This discipline that you are going to practice in University life will become your habit, and habits play significant role in giving shape to the future. The discipline gives wings to fly in sky of dreams.I and each member of Proctorial Board wish all the students a very bright future.

Public Relation Officer



The Public Relations Office is entrusted with the task of providing maintaining media coverage of all the programmes that are organized at the Glocal University campus by various departments and offices. The media coverage includes the preparation of press releases in English and Hindi languages and their further transmission to the newspapers and online media along with concerned photographs. It also looks over the arrangements for photography and videography of events and occasions and maintains a record of newspaper clippings gathered from newspapers.It is also the job of the Public Relations Office to highlight the literary, cultural, sports and academic, achievements of the University. And, to advance this objective the image of the Glocal University is projected not only in the country but across the world. The achievements and successes of the Glocal community members have always served as a motivation to the youth of the country and it is pertinent to publish the news of such achievements on national and international level. The academic standard in a University is also appraised by the achievements of its faculty members including the research work. This objective is achieved through publication of research papers in leading journals at the national and international level and their participation in seminars, conferences, workshops and symposia.
